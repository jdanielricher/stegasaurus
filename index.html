Stegasaurus is a completely anonymous, decentralized, steganography-focused image board, made on the LBRY blockchain. Steganography is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video. Users do not need to register an account before participating in the community. Feel free to click on a board below that interests you and jump right in!

Be sure to familiarize yourself with the Rules before posting, and read the FAQ if you wish to learn more about how to use the site.
